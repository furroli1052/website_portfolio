Main Idea

An invisible thread weaves through time and space,

binding two souls destined to meet. No matter how far they wander

or how many obstacles arise, their paths will cross, drawn together

by a connection written in the fabric of destiny itself.

Process

Step 1

---------------------------------

In this project, I was given the task to create a diptych poster using photos I took.

------------------------------------------------------------------

Since Valentines was happening around the corner, I decided to do something romantic. This brought me memories to an old Chinese mythology about the red string of fate. Legend has it that there is a deity that connects fated couples together with a red string.

------------------------------------------------------------------

I was watching Loki and the idea of mobius stuck to my mind, so I made the red strings into a mobius loop to symbolize eternal love

---------------------------------

Step 2

---------------------------------

However, this scene just didn’t look good. The mobius loop was awkwardly placed into the poster and the ground looked really fake and too fantasy for a modern day setting.

------------------------------------------------------------------

To help blend everything better, I gave up on the mobius loop idea. Instead, I replaced it with red string flying around a couple and connecting them into heaven. This new idea filled all the empty spaces around the subject and just looked incredibe.

------------------------------------------------------------------

The ground was changed to a rooftop to retain a sense of realism in the modern setting.

---------------------------------

Step 3

---------------------------------

But something was still missing. This project is supposed to be a diptych poster, so there should be something to connect these posters together.

------------------------------------------------------------------

Searching back into my cultural database, I realized that there was something that would perfectly fit that criteria.: “a couplet”. Couplets are mostly used to put on doors during new years for good luck. One such couplet, is poem about the red string of fate.

------------------------------------------------------------------

So I added them in to create my final poster.
