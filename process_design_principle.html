Main Idea

My design principle is simple: let your mind soar,

let your creativity shine, and embrace freedom in all you create.

In every design I craft, my goal is to ignite your imagination

and push creative boundaries.

Process

Step 1

---------------------------------

In this project, I was given the task to use AI to create 3 posters that showcased me as a designer.

------------------------------------------------------------------

I am a very expressive person in my artworks, I decided to utilize AI’s ability to mimic all these different styles from reference photos. The idea was to take expressive action filled poses of me in a photoshoot and use that AI function to create multiple stylized version of me.

---------------------------------

Step 2

---------------------------------

After generating all these images using AI, I couldn’t decide a single one. All of these AI generated images looked too good. So the problem I’m facing now is how to blend all these portraits together.

------------------------------------------------------------------

My thoughts went to mirrors, shattered glass, cracks in the wall, etc… In my eyes a vision started to form of cutting parts of the portraits and mixing and matching it with other AI generated portraits together in the form of shattered glass.

------------------------------------------------------------------

Each glass would represent an version of the expressive style within me.

---------------------------------

Step 3

---------------------------------

But something was still missing. How do I add that depth into this work?

------------------------------------------------------------------

Using my previous photoshop knowledge, I quickly realized the solution: Overlays.

------------------------------------------------------------------

So I added overlays such as fire sparks, different light leaks, did some adjustment layers to help with the color correcting, and in the end added some broken glass overlays to make it seem like my portrait was breaking out from the glass

---------------------------------
